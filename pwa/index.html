Olive's Bark Logger (browser edition)

This page measures sound levels in your browser. Audio is processed in memory and never recorded, stored, or uploaded. Events (timestamps and levels only) are saved locally in your browser. Works offline. Keep this tab open and in the foreground while monitoring: a backgrounded or locked tab cannot monitor reliably, and any such interruptions are recorded as monitoring gaps in your report.
